The Burning Hot Apu Initiative

The Burning Hot Apu Initiative (TBHAI) is all about burning $APU tokens.

By doing so they will be removed from the supply forever.

Our first events will be the Comfy Poker Table and Apu's Lucky Lottery.

The Comfy Poker Table

Let's hang out and play some Popoker with frens.

The Comfy Poker Table is part of our Burning Hot Apu initiative.

Playing Pooker (Pee Pee Poo Poo) is a great way to unite frens and burn some $APU.

How does the Apu Poker work?

Every here and then frens can join the Comfy Poker Table.

Dates will be announced via Twitter or Blog.

Everybody has to pay a small entry fee to join the tournament.

30 % of those go to the winner.

69 % of those will be converted to $APU and then burned.

1 % of those will be used for transaction fees.

We are still in research phase to find the right provider / automation via smart contracts.

It ain't much but it's honest work.

Rules can vary for each game!

How does Apu's Lucky Lottery work?
