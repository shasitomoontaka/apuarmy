How to Build Your Own Apu

  1. Click on the thumbnail you want to add.
  2. Drag & drop to place it.
  3. Use the small buttons to flip / rotate / adjust / delete.
  4. Click "Hide" or "Show" to hide / show the buttons.
  5. Download your Apu.
  6. Post your Apu on X.com with #ApuArmy and grab some $ARMY
