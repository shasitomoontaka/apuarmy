How to Play Mommy Rage

  1. Press the "Start Game" button to begin.
  2. Control Apu using the arrow keys on your keyboard or the buttons provided.
  3. Collect as many Nuggies as possible.
  4. The more Nuggies you collect, the angrier Mommy gets!
  5. Consume a Monster to calm Mommy down a bit, but it will cost you a Nuggie.
  6. Post your screenshot on X.com with #ApuArmy and grab some $ARMY.
